How to Use MindfulMingle

1. Set Your Challenge

Choose a duration for your silence or focused interaction challenge. Start with just a few minutes and work your way up!

2. Stay Silent

During the challenge, try not to speak. Use this time for reflection, active listening, or focused work.

3. Track Your Progress

If you succeed, celebrate and extend your challenge. If you speak, the app will adjust your time. Every attempt helps you grow!

4. Connect and Share

Invite friends, family, or colleagues to join your challenges. Share your achievements and inspire others on their mindfulness journey.

Discover Party Mode: Your Social Superpower!

Feeling anxious at social gatherings? Party Mode turns silence into your secret weapon! With fun, non-verbal challenges and ice-breakers, you'll connect with others in a low-pressure, exciting way. No small talk required – let your actions and presence speak volumes!
